The Maritime Crew's repertoire covers the globe, including traditional and modern shanties and songs of the sea from New Zealand and worldwide.

Shanties are traditionally working songs, providing a measured beat that kept the time for the sailors performing tasks aboard ship. From lowering the anchor to raising a mainsail, there was shanty with the rhythm for the job.

Off the clock, the fo'c'sle shanty was the song sung for enjoyment. It covered all the ground the shanty didn't, from ballad to dance.

Here you'll find a selection of well known shanties sung by the Crew. You're as likely to hear these at a performance as at the brew tent of a folk festival.
